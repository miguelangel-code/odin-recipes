Spooky Swedish Halloween Ginger Cookies

Spooky Swedish Halloween Ginger Cookies are thin, crisp and perfectly spiced. These cut-out cookies combine the warm flavors of cinnamon, ginger, cardamom, and cloves for a treat that’s both aromatic and festive. Shaped into classic Halloween designs and finished with a simple icing or served plain, these cookies are as beautiful as they are delicious.



Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Dark brown sugar
  • Light corn syrup
  • Cinnamon
  • Ground ginger
  • Cardamon
  • Cloves
  • Milk
  • Baking soda
  • Butter
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Combine flour, baking soda, salt and spices in the work bowl of a food processor. Pulse to combine.
  2. Step 2: Add butter and pulse until sandy in texture, about 15 seconds. With the machine running, pour the milk and syrup through the feed tube. Process until a dough forms.
  3. Step 3: Divide the dough into 3 equal pieces and roll each piece between 2 pieces of parchment paper until thin. Freeze the rolled dough until firm, about 15 minutes.
  4. Step 4: On a lightly floured surface, cut the dough into shapes.
  5. Step 5: Transfer the shapes to parchment lined baking sheets leaving at least an inch between each cookie.
  6. Step 6: Bake at 325 for 15 minutes. Cool completely before decorating or simply serve them plain.
